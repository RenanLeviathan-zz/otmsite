This game consists in a matrix with 6 rows and 6 columns, whose positions you have to dominate. There are 2 players: CPU (blue) and you (white). You have to fill the cell which aren't marked yet by rolling the dice (Pressing the Roll button). The winner is the one who have dominated all the columns when the time is up.
